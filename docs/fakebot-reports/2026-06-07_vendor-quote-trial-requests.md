## Universal Requirements To Send Vendors

We are building Georgia Land Finder, a parcel-screening and low-cost land discovery platform starting in Georgia and expanding nationally.

Required capabilities:

1. Parcel boundary geometry for all 159 Georgia counties.
2. API or tile access suitable for web map overlays.
3. Ability to cache/store parcel geometries in our backend, or clear terms on cache limits.
4. Display rights for parcel boundaries in a paid SaaS product.
5. Parcel attributes: APN/PIN, situs address, acreage, land use, owner/tax mailing fields if permitted.
6. Zoning data or zoning enrichment where applicable.
7. Bulk export or county/statewide preload option.
8. Pricing for startup/free-trial tier and paid production tier.
9. Rate limits, SLA, update cadence, and coverage gaps for Georgia.
10. Terms for showing owner names/tax fields to end users.
