## Top 10 must-have features
1. Parcel boundary overlays - Pro.
2. County/city boundaries - Starter.
3. FEMA flood overlay - Pro.
4. Zoning/land-use overlays where available - Pro.
5. Parcel/tax-card detail sections - Investor.
6. Off-market scoring - Investor, Pro preview.
7. Source registry and health/audit logs - Admin.
8. Change detection for source/status/price/tax fields - Investor alerts.
9. Saved searches and alert rules - Pro/Investor.
10. Exportable lead cards and map-visible leads - Pro/Investor.

## Top 10 later features
1. Sold comps and price-per-acre comparisons.
2. RESO/IDX/MLS connectors.
3. Owner mailing exports with license controls.
4. Soil/crop/ag suitability analytics.
5. Elevation/slope/buildability scoring.
6. Environmental risk cards.
7. Team/admin workflow.
8. Bulk parcel enrichment jobs.
9. Deal pipeline reminders.
10. Report builder with maps and due-diligence checklist.

## Tier matrix
- Free Tier: 5-10 sample listings, dashboard/map preview, limited source links, upgrade prompts.
- Starter: full curated listing database, source links, county/city boundaries, basic alerts.
- Pro: exports, saved leads/notes, parcel boundaries, flood/zoning/opportunity overlays, change history.
- Investor: owner/tax-card data where licensed, off-market candidates, agency workflows, daily high-fit alerts, bulk exports.
